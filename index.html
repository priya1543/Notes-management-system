About Us

Notefy is an notes management system where you can organise your notes and share notes.

Here comes the services you can avail from us.

Sign Up/ Log In
You can register through sign up or login into your account through login page.
Dashboard
In dashboard your details are likely to be displayed.
Uploads
Uploads displays the notes you have uploaded and option to upload new document
Downloads
Downloads displays the notes which are saved from other users.
Search
Search can be used to search notes based on topic,subjects and author.
Feedback
Through feedback you can share your thoughts on Notefy
